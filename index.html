About Us

SDG (Software Development Group) of NISER is a subset of the coding club. We are a community of passionate people who prefer a code-first approach to solving problems.

As a part of the coding club, we do everything from organising talks to coding competitions. And as a part of the SDG, we make various projects, specially aimed at making our lives in NISER easier and more fun. As a community, we believe in coding our problems away.
